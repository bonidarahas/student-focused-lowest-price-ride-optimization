Uber Replica Student Policy

Students get a 15% discount on rides when they verify their student email.

The system compares prices from nearby drivers and tries to assign the lowest available price to verified students.

Ride cancellation is free within 2 minutes of booking. After 2 minutes, a cancellation fee may apply.

Drivers are matched based on distance, availability, rating, and estimated pickup time.

Premium rides do not qualify for student discounts.
